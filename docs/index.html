The Ethics of AI in Healthcare

As someone interested in the intersection of machine learning/artificial intelligence and biology, I am excited by its transformative potential in advancing our understanding of various biological phenomena, as well as for revolutionizing diagnostics and personalized medicine. However, with these advances come significant ethical responsibilities that scientists and engineers must always consider.

Through this website, I hope to engage the community in my interest in the ethics of AI in healthcare research and technology. Here, I present a historical overview of research in the field, some case studies and ethical dilemmas they presented, as well as the engineering implications we can learn from these case studies. This resource is designed to help fellow engineers and researchers navigate these complex issues while advancing innovation in healthcare technology
